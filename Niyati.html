Niyati: Skincare
re-defined

Project overview

The project started with us finding problems and gaps in our daily tasks and services around us. We then mind-mapped, conducted user surveys and interviews, looked at possible idea, used ideation and prioritizing techniques, mapped a service blueprint and concept map.
And that’s how my team and I came up with the brand service Niyati, a service that unlocks the potential of every skin.

Branding Service Design System Design

Duration: 6 weeks Team type: 6 members

What problems do we aim to solve?

  • Trial and error in finding the right products
  • Unaware of side effects of skincare products
  • Falling prone to false advertising
  • Unawareness of ingredients in their skincare
  • Insecurities that are indirectly promoted

Our solution

Niyati bridges the gap between the brand and the consumer. we do not sell our own products and overpopulate the vast world of skincare. instead we recommend existing products in the market that cater to our user’s needs. Niyati helps consumers navigate through trial and error and strives to removes the error from the equation.

We approached our solution keeping these key emotions in mind. we want to empower our consumer’s individuality with compassion in our hearts. Everyone has some skincare problem or the other. its the most sensitive and visible organ. We strive to help to reduce their insecurities and increase their self esteem through our service.

A personalized kit that has a unique routine just for you!
Oh and bonus, it turns into a stand! Isn’t that cool!
